GOVERNMENT SCHOLARSHIPS

★ National Overseas Scholarship Scheme by the Indian Government

Eligibility
To be eligible, the applicant must -

  • Belong to Scheduled Castes, Denotified Nomadic and Semi-Nomadic Tribes, Landless Agricultural Laborers, and Traditional Artisans.
  • Scored 55% marks in under graduation (for those applying to Masters) or 55% in Post-Graduation (for those applying for Ph.D.).
  • Be below 35 years of age.
  • Have a combined family income that is less than Rs. 6, 00,000./li>

★ Fulbright-Nehru Master's Fellowships

Eligibility
To be eligible, the applicant must -

  • Candidate must have four-year bachelor's degree or a completed master's degree or a full-time postgraduate diploma from a recognized Indian institution
  • The candidate must have a minimum of 55% or higher in an undergraduate degree.
  • Must possess good leadership skills and have experience in community service.
  • 3 years of work experience apart from being committed to returning to India and contributing to their community.

★ Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degrees

Eligibility
