ABOUT US

A large segment of India’s potential workforce is unemployable. With 76.04% literacy rate and an increasing number of dropouts failing to enrol or complete any form of higher education. Lack of know-how about education funding schemes is one of the key contributors to this effect. myScholar is endeavouring to bridge the gap between scholarship providers and scholarship seekers

India’s largest scholarship network with the vision to make quality education accessible for all. A brainchild of IIT, IIM, and BITS Pilani alumni, myScholar aggregates global scholarship information.We provide easy access to scholarships with end-to-end application support to seekers and end-to-end management and monitoring of scholarship programs to providers
