GOVERNMENT SCHOLARSHIPS

★ NSP

Eligibility
To be eligible, the applicant must -

  • be an Indian national
  • be studying in Class 11th and class 12th
  • have an annual family income of less than INR 2.50 lakh
  • amount of Rs.12000 will be awarded

★ SSP

Eligibility
To be eligible, the applicant must -

  • be an Indian national
  • be studying in Class 11th and class 12th
  • have an annual family income of less than INR 1.50 lakh
  • amount of Rs.10000 will be awarded

PRIVATE SCHOLARSHIPS

★ Colgate Scholarship

Eligibility
To be eligible, the applicant must -

  • Applicant must be Indian citizen
  • Must be enrolled in Class 11th and class 12th at a recognised school in India
  • Must have scored at least 75% in previous class exam
  • amount of Rs.14000 will be awarded
